### Landing Page Copy Contract

**Intro card title:** "About This Project" (unchanged per D-13)

**Intro card body direction** (per D-14):
1. Open with what wind-powered desalination is (1-2 sentences)
2. Describe the senior design context -- Fall 2025 through Spring 2026, 10,000-person municipality scenario
3. Describe what the three systems represent technically:
   - Mechanical = hydraulic drive (HPU, manifold, hydraulic motors driving pumps)
   - Electrical = battery/tank storage with electrical conversion
   - Hybrid = fixed preset combining hydraulic and electrical approaches
4. State what students should explore and compare
5. No references to AI, automated tools, or data sources
6. Maintain contributor line below the body paragraph

**Mechanical system card description** (per D-16):
- Remove "wind-driven pumps"
- Describe hydraulic drive architecture: HPU driving vertical turbine pump and plunger pump via manifold and hydraulic motors
- Keep "Click Explore to view cost, land area, and energy data" opening pattern

**Hybrid system card description** (per D-15):
- Remove "build a custom system" and "select one piece of equipment for each process stage"
- Describe as a fixed preset combining hydraulic mechanical and electrical approaches
- Keep "Click Explore to view..." opening pattern

**Electrical system card description:**

## Interaction Contracts

### Slider Behavior

| Slider ID | Update Mode | Direct Input | Marks | Tooltip |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|-------|---------|
| `slider-time-horizon` | `mouseup` (no change) | `False` (add) | `{1: "1yr", 25: "25yr", 50: "50yr"}` (no change) | `always_visible: True` (no change) |
| `slider-battery` | `mouseup` (no change) | `False` (add) | `{0: "100% Tank", 0.5: "50/50", 1: "100% Battery"}` (replace empty) | `always_visible: True, placement: "bottom"` (change from False) |
| `slider-tds` | `mouseup` (change from drag) | `False` (add) | no change | no change |
| `slider-depth` | `mouseup` (change from drag) | `False` (add) | no change | no change |

### Loading Spinner

- Wrapper: single `dcc.Loading` around the div containing `row1` and `row2` (the 2x2 chart grid)
- Spinner type: `"default"` (Bootstrap circle spinner)
- Does NOT wrap the control panel or legend row
- Spinner appears during any chart callback execution (all four charts update together)

### First-Visit Banner

- Component: `dbc.Alert(color="info", is_open=True)` controlled by a `dcc.Store`
- Placement: inserted before `control_panel` in the charts layout return value
- Dismiss trigger: callback with `Input` on all four slider value properties; any slider interaction sets store to `{"dismissed": True}` and `is_open` to `False`
- Persistence: session-only (`dcc.Store` default); banner reappears on page reload
- No X button (`dismissable=False`) -- dismissal is slider-driven as stated in banner text
- Print: banner should have `className="no-print"` so it does not appear in PDF export
